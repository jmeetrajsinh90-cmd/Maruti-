Our History

Maruti Suzuki India Limited, formerly known as Maruti Udyog Limited, was established in 1981 as a joint venture between the Government of India and Suzuki Motor Corporation, Japan. The company’s first car, the Maruti 800, was launched in 1983 and revolutionized the Indian automotive industry by making affordable cars accessible to the masses.

Over the decades, Maruti Suzuki expanded its portfolio with popular models like Alto, Swift, Baleno, WagonR, Celerio, and many more. With a strong focus on quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction, Maruti Suzuki became India’s largest car manufacturer and continues to hold the leadership position in the market.

Today, Maruti Suzuki operates with a wide network of dealerships and service centers across India, offering vehicles that meet diverse needs — from entry-level hatchbacks to premium sedans and SUVs. With innovation, trust, and value at its core, Maruti Suzuki remains the most preferred automobile brand for millions of Indians.

Milestones Timeline

  • 1981 Company founded as Maruti Udyog Limited.
  • 1983 Launch of the iconic Maruti 800.
  • 2000s Expansion with Alto, WagonR, Swift; rapid dealership growth.
  • 2010s Nexa channel launch; premium models & safety features.
  • 2020s SUVs like Brezza & Grand Vitara; focus on tech, safety and efficiency.
